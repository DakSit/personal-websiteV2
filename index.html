About Me

I love creating things. Since I was a child I've been making up ideas for board games, video games, and fantastical (if sometimes impossible) worlds. That love for creation has made me begin to focus on Front-End and Web Development because I feel like it is one of the coolest ways to be able to create things people love to interact with. I'm still just starting out my career but I'm hoping I can find my place in a team, work consistently and diligently, and learn from people smarter than I am. That's me professionally.

In regards to what I'm like outside of the office, I love to run Dungeons and Dragons for my friends every week, lift weights as much as I can, and learn new recipes for my meal prep sessions. I'm hoping to one day be the guy who bikes to work. If you took the time to read this bio, thank you. I hope it brought you as much joy seeing my work as it brought me happiness to have created it.

Combat Clicks

Combat Clicks is a Rogue-Like Clicker Game I developed based around turn-based combat and using buttons as the way the player interacts with foes. It includes small amounts of jQuery but is almost entirely vanilla JS. It is what started my love of the language and what it could be used to accomplish.
Note: Given the spirit of clicker games, this is the only of my projects that is NOT mobile-friendly.
